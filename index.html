About Me

Nine years professional UX design and development experience. I put an emphasis on collaboration, creativity, and pixel-perfect implementation. From project conceptualization to final production , I try to bridge the gap between design and engineering teams.

In my free time, I'm an avid movie watcher and science fiction fan and a sucker for the latest and greatest front-end framework. Keeping up with the web is always a challenge, but I find working-through ideas and prototyping for designers is a constant push to extend my javascript skills and keeps me enjoying my work.

Like anyone else that sits at a computer all day, I try to balance my career and personal life as best I can and wind down every night with a long walk, or a casual run with my wife and dog.
